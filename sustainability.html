Environmental sustainability

We respect natural resources.

We do not have the ability of producing natural resources, so we make the best out of our consumption on energy, raw materials, air, and water.

We invest in efficiency on machinery and buildings that save electricity and gas.

We minimize material waste by constantly reviewing and revising every production process.

We make effort to eliminate environmental risks by utilizing filters and scrubbers against air emissions, and building sewage plant for pollutants in industrial wastewater.

Automation for manufacturing process.

We favor automation for manufacturing process in order to keep our employees from potential toxic chemicals. Furthermore, the remote controlled machines and warehouses increase efficiency and reduce potential risks of accidents.
