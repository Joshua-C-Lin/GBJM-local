Exclusive tour: becoming GBJM & our Vision, Mission, and Value

Genuine & Bold. Joint & Modern.

GBJM was introduced to luxury metal accessories in European high fashion industry in 2013, with the revolutionary concept incorpo- rated with great inheritance of exceptional technology, exquisite craftsmanship, and ingenuity model.

Focusing on impeccable details on manufacturing process, and possibilities offered by new methods and technologies, we have made up an exceptional integrated supply chain, built over time through entrepreneurial spirit and the green-mannered production lines, whose reliability is guaranteed by the transparency that distinguishes us. Becoming one of the major partners of European high fashion brands, we mastered in luxury management and customer service rooted in combination of timeless perspective and subtle attitudes which form an unique classic signature. We explore the strength of joint capacity based on uncompromising quality, speaking out in a modern sustainable way to the world.

We cherish every step in this unique journey and make every endeavor with acute awareness yet remain passionate as the core of innovation and fun. As connecting designers, curators, and consumers, we retain agility with the aim of boosting inspiration outside the box and seeking for new path to more talent, contribution, and value.

SERVICE

From concept development, material research and pro- totype shaping, to dispatching the final products across the world, GBJM specializes in precision molding and surface treatments for luxury metal accessories, and has developed innovative systematic processes ranging from Metal Injection Molding to Metalwork Surface Treat- ments and Color Solutions.

Rapid prototyping & small lot production

With the professional workshop and machinery, we deliv- er prototypes and even small-lot production for runway collections within short lead time which is crucial for the dynamic high fashion world.

Quick & efficient application

We adapt to the needs of each brand and provide the world of fashion and luxury with extensive product range with unbeatably high quality.
