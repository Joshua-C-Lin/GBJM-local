Materials & technology
We wear innovative technology everyday.

MIM-Metal Injection Molding

MIM (Metal Injection Molding) with stainless steel, which imposes few restrictions on the design end, is essentially a technology capa- ble of producing complex shape parts in both large and small quantities. It could support high-volume manufacturing of small and precision parts with tight tolerances. With producing less material wastes and performing excellent surface prior to coating process, MIM provides uniformed and precise output in a more economical and eco-friendly manner.

3D Printing

3D printing also known as additive manufacturing, is an additive technique creating less material wastage and allows ideas to be prototyped and tested without going through a production process.

PVD-Physical Vapor Deposition

PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) is a cutting-edge coating technique which provides metal accessories with corrosion resistance and iridescent effect. With the strong support of great inheritance of PVD technology, GBJM excelled to make it sustainable and low environmental impact .

Spray painting & Enamel Workshop

Spray painting is the key technology of GBJM containing features of high-thickness, heat and oxidation resistance, coating luxury metal accessories with saturated color in high fashion brands. We creates a systematic database for color formula and develop- ment, which delivers the most precise visual performance on metal with the highest level of stability and durability.
